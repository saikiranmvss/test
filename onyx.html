Onyx

Crafted from Calcite, this stratified stone showcases breathtaking formations in a vibrant and expansive color palette. The onyx formations manifest in either "cloud-like" shapes or striking striations, determined by whether the onyx slab is Cross-Cut or Vein-Cut. The aesthetic allure of onyx surpasses that of any other material, and its ability to be backlit, owing to its inherent semi-translucency, makes a bold statement in any residential or commercial project.

Onyx finds its ideal application in interior spaces, serving as an exquisite choice for commercial and residential bars, bathroom vanities, wall cladding, and decorative elements. As a softer and more porous stone, onyx shares similarities with marble, rendering it susceptible to both chemical and abrasion exposure. Therefore, it is recommended for interior use to maintain its beauty and longevity.
